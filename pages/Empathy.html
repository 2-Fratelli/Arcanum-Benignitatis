What is empathy?

Empathy is the capacity to understand and share the feelings and experiences of other people. It allows us to perceive and interpret how others feel in a given context. It also helps us to comprehend the emotions, motivations or the behavior of the people around us from their perspective.

Empathy is the base of human connections, it drives compassionate actions, builds stronger relationships, and reduces conflict among people.

Empathy is applied everywere. It is essential in both, personal and profesional relationships. It fosters communication among people and create trusted spaces for sharing fears, problems, etc.
